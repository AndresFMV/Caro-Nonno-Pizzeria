Pizza 3 Quesos al Disco:
Ingredients: 
- Pizza dough
- 100 grams of mozzarella cheese
- 100 grams of cheddar cheese
- 100 grams of gorgonzola cheese
- A handful of fresh basil leaves
- 2 cloves of garlic, minced
- Extra virgin olive oil
- Extra mozzarella cheese

Instructions:

1. Preheat your oven to its highest setting.
2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
3. Spread a thin layer of olive oil over the top of the dough.
4. Sprinkle the mozzarella, cheddar, and gorgonzola cheese evenly over the dough.
5. Bake in the preheated oven until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
6. Remove from the oven and immediately top with fresh basil leaves.
7. Serve hot and enjoy your Pizza 3 Quesos al Disco!
