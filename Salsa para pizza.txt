Salsa para Pizza:
Ingredients: 
- A large onion, finely chopped
- A glass of tomato sauce
- Two medium tomatoes, diced
- A large bell pepper, diced
- Condiments to taste (salt, pepper, oregano, basil)
- 2 cloves of garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon of sugar (optional)
- 1 tablespoon of olive oil

Instructions:

1. In a pan, heat a tablespoon of olive oil and saut√© the finely chopped onion and minced garlic until they become translucent.
2. Add the diced tomatoes and bell pepper to the pan and cook until they soften.
3. Pour in the tomato sauce and add the condiments (salt, pepper, oregano, basil). You can also add a teaspoon of sugar if you prefer a slightly sweet sauce. Let it simmer for a few minutes until it thickens.
